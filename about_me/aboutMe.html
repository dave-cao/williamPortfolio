"Draw drunk, edit sober" - Someone Said

William Heng is a Buddhist monk turned storyboard artist.

He explored the Buddhist myth and the stories of people from many parts of the world.

However, during his childhood, he lost his capacity for self-expression in this conservative and purist way of life and so he left monkhood.

He found his love for art again, and now strives to become a great visual storyteller.
